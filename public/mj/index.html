Affliations / LifeTech Holdings

LifeTech Holdings Sdn Bhd is Malaysia's premier IT Solution and Service provider, pioneering and delivering state-of-the-art System Integration Services, Outsourcing Services & Management, while providing a 24x7 Help Desk Service and Software Development & Support.

Today's competitive environment drives us to further improve the way we do business in an effort to provide our customers with high quality products and unparalleled services. This is demonstrated through our ever-expanding list of high quality clients.

Since our establishment in 1989, we have grown by providing total network solutions, wireless communication technologies and systems integration and consultancy through our subsidiaries in Malaysia and worldwide alliance partners.

And with our key global partnerships, we are able to combine our resources to provide proven technology integrated into solutions for our clients.
